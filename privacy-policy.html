# Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 2026 ## Overview WeirdLand Rancher is currently a single-player prototype game developed by JustAWeirdMango. At this time, WeirdLand Rancher does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share any personal information from players. ## Information Collection The game does not require users to create an account or provide personal information. The developer does not collect: * Names * Email addresses * Passwords * Payment information * Location information * Personal identifiers ## Save Data Game save data is stored locally on the player's device. The developer does not receive, store, or access this data. ## Discord Rich Presence If enabled, the game may communicate with Discord Rich Presence to display gameplay information such as: * Current game status * Playtime * In-game progress * Other gameplay-related information This information is processed through Discord's services and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. ## Third-Party Services The game may be distributed through third-party platforms such as itch.io and Discord. These platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies. ## Future Changes As WeirdLand Rancher is currently in active development, future versions may introduce online features. If personal information is ever collected, this Privacy Policy will be updated before such features are released. ## Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the official WeirdLand Rancher itch.io page.
